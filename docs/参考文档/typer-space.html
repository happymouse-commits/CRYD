Why Typer exists.

In February 2026, ChatGPT started showing ads to free users. They made $100 million in six weeks. Think about what that means for a chat app. Something that knows what you’re curious about, worried about, planning.

Turns out your Mac can already do most of what ChatGPT does. Every Mac with Apple Silicon runs AI models right on the chip. No cloud, no account, and your chats stay on your Mac.

Typer is a chat app that puts all of that in one place. No ads. Just your Mac.

Yes. Typer is fully functional without internet. Unlike the standard AI you use online, Typer runs AI on your computer. No internet is needed to send your chat to be processed by AI. Your chat does not reach AI owned by a company on a server far away. No internet is needed to send the answers back to you. With Typer, it all happens inside your Mac. You get the power of AI without internet.

Typer does have the ability to search the web, but is fully functional without it.

You don’t need to know anything about AI to use Typer. Download the app, open it and start chatting. That’s it.

Behind the scenes, Typer picks the best AI model for your specific Mac and sets everything up. You don’t need to configure anything or know what a “model” is. Typer comes with one of the smallest (but worthwhile) models available and is always ready to chat. Right out of the box.

Other local AI tools expect you to choose an AI, adjust settings and set things up yourself. That’s great if you enjoy tinkering, but most people just want to chat.

Typer does all of that for you. Download it, open it, start talking. It picks the smartest model your Mac can run and keeps it up to date as better ones come out. No configuration, no decisions, no manual downloads.
